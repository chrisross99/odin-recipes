Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipes

These chocolate chip cookies will become an immediate family favorite

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 Cups of Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Butter
  • 3/4 Cup of Granulated Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup of Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 Package of Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together in a mixing bowl
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, then mix togehter into cohesive dough
  4. Fold in package of chocolate chips
  5. Roll dough into 1 1/2 tablespoon balls, place on prepped baking sheet
  6. Bake 9 - 11 minutes, allow to cool on wire rack for at least 10 minutes
